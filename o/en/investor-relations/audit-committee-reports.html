Summary of the 61st Audit Committee’s meeting

Summary of the 61st Audit Committee’s meeting “3rd for 2017” to be presented to the Chairman and Members of the Board of Directors of ADIB – UAE

Following is a summary of the most significant issues of the 61st ADIB - UAE Audit Committee meeting “3rd for 2017”:

1. Audit Committee meeting was held on 07.08.2017 to work through the meeting’s agenda and discuss the following items;

  • Approving minutes of the 60th Committee meeting (2nd for 2017) held on 07/05/2017.
  • Follow up of the recommendations and resolutions of the previous meeting of the Audit Committee.
  • Review the draft financial statements of the bank and discuss with Finance department and the external auditors their most significant observations related to the Financial Statements as of 30/06/2017 and Audit Committee members’ meeting with the external auditors in the absence of the bank’s management, if needed
  • Discuss most significant issues in Compliance and Corporate Governance Department.
  • Reviewing the assumptions of AML alerts and comparing them with other banks and ADIB, UAE. Also, presenting to the committee an aging of the alerts from receiving them till closure and inviting managers from Compliance department.
  • Discuss and approve Whistle Blowing Policy.
  • Discussion of ARR reports.
  • Discussion of the progress of the annual audit Plan for 2017.
  • Discuss and approve new Audit and Risk Review Charter.
  • Discussion of the value of the customers’ collaterals against their credit facilities.
  • Inviting Head of Retail Business to discuss the actions taken in Smouha branch case and the corrective action plan to prevent the recurrence of similar cases in future
  • Any other issues

2. Most important resolutions and recommendations of the Audit Committee were;

The Committee discussed the agenda items and the most important resolutions and recommendations were:

  • Approving minutes of the 60th committee meeting (2nd for 2017) held on 7/5/2017.
  • Approving the draft financial statements of the bank as of 30/6/2017 and submitting them to the Board of Directors for approval.
  • Approval of the Whistle Blowing policy after amending the article of “Assuming of Complaints” to be directed to a special email including the Head of ARR and the Head of Compliance
  • Initial approval on the new charter of the Audit and Risk Review department subject to circulation for any amendments required by Mr. Mohamed El Dahan to the other committee members by Mr. Amr Samy.
  • Committee members have no objection to raise the threshold limits of the AML alerts as per the analysis of the compliance department.

Regards,

Farid ElBilbeise Mohamed El Dahan Mohamed Youssef Amr A. Halim
Chairman Member Member Secretary
