      Card Protection:

       
      Purchase Protection & Extended Warranty
      Customers are eligible for a refund up to USD 5,000 per item if purchases are accidentally broken, stolen, or damaged, within 365 days from date of purchase.
      Manufacturer or retailer’s warranty will be automatically extended on a range of goods for a combined total of 24 months.

       
      Credit Shield Takaful Protection
      Optional Takaful cover provided to protect in the event of involuntary unemployment, death, permanent disability, critical illness and accidental medical expenses.
