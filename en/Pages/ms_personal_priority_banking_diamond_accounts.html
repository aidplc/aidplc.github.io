    ​​
    Whether you’re looking to take care of your day-to-day banking needs or to make a one-off investment, ADIB Diamond offers a wide range of accounts so we can help you make the most out of your money. 

     

    ADIB accounts include ADIB Current account, Ghina ​Savings account, Short Term Investments​ and Recurring Investment Account (Time Deposits)​.​

    ADIB Diamond Visa Infinite Debit Card 
    Available in AED currency, your ADIB Diamond Visa Infinite Debit Card comes with a daily withdrawal limit of up to AED 40,000 and a daily spend limit of up to AED 100,000 when using your​ debit card in the UAE and abroad. This branded card also opens up a world of distinguished lifestyle ben​efits​. 

    ​Ladies also enjoy their own ADIB Dana Visa Infinite Debit Card . 


    ADIB Diamond Cheque book free for life 
    For your current account, your ADIB Diamond Cheque book is specially designed and available in AED currency. 

    Bank in Luxury at ADIB Gold Centers
    As an ADIB Diamond member you can visit any of the ADIB Gold Centers in branches where you can meet your Dedicated Relationship Manager who will take care of your banking needs while you sit back and relax. 

    Click here for a list of Gold Centers.

    ADIB Gold Centers include live screens tracking the financial markets and complimentary internet access. ​​​

    Eligibility Criteria 

    Minimum Deposit: AED 750,000 or

    Minimum Salary: AED 80,000 or 

    ADIB Home Finance of AED 4,000,000 or more
