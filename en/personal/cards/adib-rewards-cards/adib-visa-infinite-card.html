      Multi-trip travel cover
      Travelling might be your priority while protecting you is ours! That’s why your ADIB Edge Card covers you, your spouse and up to 5 children (age limit of 75 per individual), while travelling with the following:
      • Medical cover
        • Emergency Medical expenses up to US $1,000,000
        • Evacuation and Repatriation expenses up to US $500,000
        • Hospital Daily Indemnity up to US $3,000
        ​
      • Personal cover
        • Personal Accident up to US $1,000,000
        • Personal Liability up to US $500,000
        ​
      • Travel inconvenience cover
        • Trip Cancellation or Curtailment or Abandonment up to US $5,000
        • Delayed departure up to US $900
        • Baggage delay up to US $500
        • Loss of personal belongings up to US $2,500
        ​
